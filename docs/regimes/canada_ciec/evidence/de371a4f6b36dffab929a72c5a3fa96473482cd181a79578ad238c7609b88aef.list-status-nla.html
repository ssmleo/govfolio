Public Declaration of Agreed Compliance Measures (Section 29)
No Longer Applicable
Dawn Lorraine Farrell · Chief Executive Officer of the Canadian Major Projects Office
Description

As part of compensation from previous positions, I currently own restricted share units of Portland General Electric Co. and shares of The Chemours Co. These shares cannot be immediately divested because the Portland General Electric Co. shares have not reached their vesting date, and the shares of The Chemours Co. are in a black-out period.
It has been agreed that until these assets can be divested, the corporate books have been updated so that any communication or other information relating to these assets will not be sent to me but to the Trustee of my Blind Trust.
During such time, the Conflict of Interest and Ethics Commissioner and I have agreed that, as required by section 21 of the Conflict of Interest Act, I will recuse myself from any discussion or decision in relation to Portland General Electric Co. and The Chemours Co. and their subsidiaries, aimed at preventing any opportunity to further my own private interests, while I exercise my official powers, duties and functions as a public office holder.
I understand that recusal means that I must remove myself from the room, physically or virtually as the case may be. I also agree not to have any communication with government officials in relation to any matter or decision-making processes which I know or reasonably should know to affect my private interests.
I must so inform the Conflict of Interest and Ethics Commissioner and, where appropriate, make a public declaration of recusal pursuant to subsection 25(1) of the Act.
The shares of Portland General Electric Co. will be immediately sold in an arm’s-length transaction within 10 business days following the date of vesting.
The shares of Chemours Co. will be immediately sold in an arm’s length transaction within 10 business days of the date of the end of the black-out period.
This compliance measure will remain in force until the Commissioner notifies me in writing that it is no longer applicable following receipt of proof of divestment of the shares of Portland General Electric Co. and The Chemours Co.
