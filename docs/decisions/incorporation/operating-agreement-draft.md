# SINGLE-MEMBER LLC OPERATING AGREEMENT (DRAFT — HAVE REVIEWED BEFORE SIGNING)

**govfolio [LLC name TBD], a Wyoming Limited Liability Company**

*This is a standard single-member Wyoming LLC template synthesized for this situation.
It is not legal advice. Have a Wyoming-licensed attorney or the formation service
review before signing — most formation bundles (Firstbase/doola/Northwest) include a
review pass at no extra cost.*

## Article 1 — Formation
1.1. This Operating Agreement is entered into as of [DATE] by [FOUNDER FULL LEGAL NAME]
("Member"), being the sole member of [LLC NAME], LLC (the "Company"), a limited
liability company organized under the Wyoming Limited Liability Company Act, Wyo. Stat.
§ 17-29-101 et seq.

1.2. The Company's Articles of Organization were filed with the Wyoming Secretary of
State on [DATE], assigned filing number [FILING NUMBER].

1.3. The Company's registered agent is [REGISTERED AGENT NAME], located at
[REGISTERED AGENT ADDRESS].

## Article 2 — Purpose
2.1. The Company is organized to engage in any lawful business, including but not
limited to the operation of a financial-disclosure data aggregation, tracking, and
alerting service (govfolio.io), and any other lawful purpose the Member determines.

## Article 3 — Member
3.1. The Member is the sole member of the Company and owns 100% of the membership
interest.

3.2. No additional members may be admitted without the Member's written consent.

## Article 4 — Management
4.1. The Company is member-managed. The Member has full, exclusive authority to manage
the business and affairs of the Company, including the authority to open bank accounts,
enter contracts, engage service providers (payment processors, hosting, contractors),
and act on behalf of the Company in all matters.

4.2. The Member may delegate day-to-day authority to officers or agents but retains
ultimate management authority.

## Article 5 — Capital Contributions
5.1. The Member has contributed [$AMOUNT / description of initial contribution, e.g.
"$0 cash plus pre-existing intellectual property, source code, and business assets
related to govfolio.io"] as the initial capital contribution.

5.2. No additional contributions are required unless the Member elects to make them.

## Article 6 — Allocations and Distributions
6.1. All profits, losses, and distributions are allocated 100% to the Member.

6.2. Distributions are made at the Member's sole discretion, subject to Wyoming law
restrictions on distributions that would render the Company insolvent.

## Article 7 — Limitation of Liability
7.1. The Member is not personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of
the Company solely by reason of being a member, except as required by law (e.g. unpaid
wages, certain tax obligations, or fraud/commingling that pierces the liability shield).

7.2. The Member shall maintain the Company's funds separate from personal funds at all
times to preserve this limitation.

## Article 8 — Indemnification
8.1. The Company shall indemnify the Member against claims arising from actions taken
in good faith on behalf of the Company, to the fullest extent permitted by Wyoming law.

## Article 9 — Dissolution
9.1. The Company may be dissolved upon the Member's written election, or as otherwise
required by the Wyoming LLC Act.

9.2. Upon dissolution, assets are distributed: first to creditors, then to the Member.

## Article 10 — Amendment
10.1. This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by the Member.

## Article 11 — Governing Law
11.1. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Wyoming.
